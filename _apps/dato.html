--- title: Dato subtitle: Better menu bar clock with calendar and time zones description: Dato is a replacement for the system menu bar clock. By default, it looks exactly the same, but when you click it, you'll get a small calendar, ability to show the current time in various time zones (even with custom names), optionally show the week number, and more.
